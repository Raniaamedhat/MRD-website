What is Orascom Development real estate company?

Orascom Development is considered one of the most prominent companies in the field of real estate development in the Arab world, and since its establishment it has achieved an excellent reputation in the real estate industry.

Orascom Development aims to develop and build high-quality real estate projects in Egypt and the Gulf countries. The company is characterized by its ability to achieve the ideal balance between unique designs and advanced technology, in order to meet customer needs and provide distinguished residential and commercial spaces.

Orascom Development has a diverse portfolio of amazing projects in the Middle East, including luxury residential buildings, modern commercial malls and major commercial projects. Its innovative architectural facades and delightful interior design makes each of its projects stand out for its uniqueness and excellence.

Thanks to its skills and extensive experience, Orascom Development has achieved great success in the global real estate market. It has won many prestigious awards in the Middle East and the world, which confirms the quality and professionalism of its work.

Orascom Development is a living example of successful institutions in the real estate sector. The company deals with large-scale projects and generates huge profits for investors and added value for local communities.

Projects of Orascom Development

Orascom Development continues to achieve continuous successes and develop its sports, commercial and residential projects that meet market needs. Orascom Development is one of the leading real estate companies in the region, aiming to develop sustainable and advanced communities.
