Seldar Real Estate Development Company

What is Seldar Real Estate Development Company Seldar Misr?

Seldar Real Estate Development Company plays a leading role in the real estate investment sector in Egypt. The company was founded in 2007, and owns and develops a wide range of prestigious projects in the most prominent commercial locations in the country. Seldar specializes in combining luxury hospitality ownership and development with commercial and residential real estate.

Seldar Egypt invests in its various projects in areas such as Cairo, Alexandria, Hurghada and New Cairo. Over its long 13-year journey in the Egyptian market, Seldar Egypt has established an excellent reputation as a leading company in its field of operations.

The mission of Seldar Egypt is to pursue outstanding real estate assets with sustainable value, and to achieve strategic growth and increased profits for all stakeholders. Seldar operates through its eight wholly owned subsidiaries, which include Arab Company for Hotels and Resorts, Gulf Contracting & Construction Company, Arab Company for Investments, Sumerian Contracting & Real Estate Investment Company, Seldar Egypt for Real Estate Investment, Sanier Tourism Company, Delta Tourism and Hotels, and Sanier Human Resource Development.

The company aims to become the largest real estate investment company in the region, while maintaining unparalleled quality standards. Led by eight wholly owned subsidiaries, Seldar has advanced operations in hospitality, retail and real estate development.

It appears that Seldar Real Estate Development Company has achieved an impressive record of success and excellence, enjoying a good reputation in the real estate investment market in Egypt. The company continues to work towards achieving its future goals and developing exceptional real estate projects that meet the needs of investors and clients.

Projects of Seldar Real Estate Development Company in Egypt

Seldar Real Estate Development Company in Egypt has achieved tremendous success over the past 15 years and aims to become one of the most prominent hospitality and real estate investment companies in the country.
