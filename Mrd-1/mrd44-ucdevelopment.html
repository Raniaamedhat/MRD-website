UC Development Real Estate Company

overview of UC Development Real Estate

Recent online data has shown that UC Development Real Estate Company is a leading company in the field of real estate investment in Egypt. The company was established as an investment entity based on an alliance and integration of expertise in real estate investment, architectural engineering, marketing consulting and project management.

UC Development Real Estate Company has been operating in the Egyptian market for more than 20 years, and has been keen to provide distinguished and diverse investment projects. The company is concerned with diversifying its work and seeks to provide its customers with a variety of choices when buying.

UC Development Real Estate Company has implemented many pioneering projects in Egypt. These projects include the implementation of Union Towers Mall in 6th of October City and Solai Golf Residence compound in the New Administrative Capital.

UC Development Real Estate Company continues to excel and provide distinguished real estate solutions in Egypt's real estate investment market.

UC Development Real Estate Company Projects

UC Development Real Estate Company recently announced its investment in the New Administrative Capital by establishing 3 mixed-use projects, which will provide investors with a unique opportunity to invest and live in these remarkable places. Here are the details of UC's projects in the new capital.

New Administrative Capital

Lavin Tower Compound

The New Administrative Capital sets itself as an ideal destination for upcoming residence and experiences of lavish shopping and hospitality. Lavin Tower Compound New Capital is located in the North Ben Zayed Axis area, and applies a modern system to dispose of waste and refuse to preserve the civilized appearance of the project.

Lavin Tower Compound New Administrative Capital is a project of UC Development Real Estate Company extending over an area of 12,000 square meters. The project provides a diverse range of units, including administrative, hotel and commercial units that operate with smart card, handprint and face recognition systems.

Lavin Tower Compound New Administrative Capital has exceptional advantages, as the designs include innovative green areas and dancing fountains that give the place an aesthetic touch. The project also features state-of-the-art European lighting technology.

To enhance comfort and luxury, Lavin Tower Compound New Administrative Capital has a number of swimming pools evenly distributed within the project, in addition to special hotel services such as daily cleaning service.

New Administrative Capital

Union Tower Compound

Union Mall Tower New Administrative Capital is located directly on the first square in Downtown Town, and is connected to three main corners of the city, including the tourist walkway and the western axis of Downtown Town. The project is also characterized by its direct views of the iconic tower, which is a major tourist landmark in the Capital. In addition, Union Tower Mall New Administrative Capital is in close proximity to the North Ben Zayed Axis and the Monorail station, making access to it easy.

Union Tower New Administrative Capital includes medical, administrative and commercial units of varying sizes to suit all categories. The project was designed with unique engineering designs to be a shining star in the sky of the New Administrative Capital. The design of Union Tower New Administrative Capital is distinguished in that it is the creativity of the advisory office Hany Saad, as the design came in the form of wheat spikes, which symbolize prosperity.

UC Development Real Estate Company is one of the leading real estate companies in the Egyptian market, distinguished by its presentation of remarkable real estate projects in the New Administrative Capital. These projects range from residential to commercial, providing investors with opportunities to invest and earn profitable returns. The company also provides flexible payment and installment options that suit the different needs and financial capabilities of investors.
