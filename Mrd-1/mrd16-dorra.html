Dorra Holding Group

Dorra Holding Group for Construction and Development announces its realization of tangible and distinguished achievements in the real estate sector. The company is considered one of the leading real estate companies in the region, headquartered in the capital Riyadh.

Dorra Holding Group was founded in 1943, and since then it has achieved widespread fame thanks to a wide range of successful projects. The company operates in the fields of construction, building and development, focusing on providing unique and innovative projects.

Dorra Holding Group works hard to stimulate economic growth and infrastructure development. The company works to meet market needs by providing innovative, high quality projects at affordable prices. The company places customer satisfaction first, striving to provide excellent services and happy family experiences for its customers.
