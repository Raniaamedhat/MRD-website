About Mangroovy Residence Company

Mangroovy Residence Company is one of the leading companies in the field of urbanization and construction in Egypt. The company provides a diverse range of luxurious and modern properties, including apartments, villas, townhouses and chalets, to meet the needs of different clients.

Mangroovy El Gouna Resort is characterized by its unique location overlooking the sea directly, making it one of the luxurious tourist projects in the area. The project is located on New Zaafarana Road, and close to international airports such as Hurghada International Airport and Sharm El Sheikh Airport. It also provides a commercial area containing many shops, banking and financial services.

Mangroovy cooperated with major companies specialized in design and engineering, such as Orascom and global Campbell Gray, to ensure the highest standards of quality and innovation in project designs. The company strives hard to provide unique and exceptional projects that meet customer expectations and gain their trust.

Mangroovy is interested in providing distinguished real estate advisory services, as it provides accurate studies of the real estate market in Egypt through its expertise and experts. The company also pays great attention to choosing enchanting locations and large spaces for its projects.

With Mangroovy Residence Company, clients can enjoy a luxurious and sophisticated lifestyle, as the company strives to create communities that provide all the services and requirements that clients need.
