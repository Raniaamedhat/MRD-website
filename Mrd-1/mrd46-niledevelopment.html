Nile Business City

Nile Business City in the New Administrative Capital is characterized by its strategic location in the New Administrative Capital, and provides investment opportunities in the commercial, administrative and hotel apartment sectors. Unit areas start from 45 square meters.

Nile Business City is characterized by many features and distinctive services, such as surveillance cameras and fire detection systems to ensure safety and security throughout the project.
